Predictive Agricultural Insights

Random Forest Crop Suitability Models

By feeding soil metrics (NPK, pH) and seasonal variables (temp, rainfall) into optimized Random Forest classifiers, OptiCrop produces crop selections with outstanding precision.

Yield Forecasting & Regressor Algorithms

Regressor models estimate crop yield (tons per hectare) by evaluating location encodings, seasonal weather forecasts, and chemical inputs to ensure predictable commercial planning.

Soil K-Means Diagnostic Clustering

K-Means clustering categorizes soil health into five clusters, mapping clay or sandy profiles to optimal fertilizer prescriptions (blood meal, bone meal, muriate of potash).
